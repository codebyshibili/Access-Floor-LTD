3. Does Access Floors Trading offer customizable flooring solutions?

Yes, we provide customizable flooring options, including a variety of finishes, designs, and materials for raised access floors, carpets, SPC flooring, LVT flooring, and vinyl flooring to meet specific commercial requirements.

4. What makes SPC flooring a popular choice in the UAE?

SPC flooring is highly durable, water-resistant, and easy to maintain, making it ideal for commercial spaces in the UAE. Its ability to withstand heavy foot traffic while offering a stylish appearance adds to its appeal.

5. What is LVT flooring, and where can it be used?

Luxury Vinyl Tile (LVT) flooring is a versatile and durable option that replicates the look of natural materials like wood or stone. It’s perfect for offices, retail spaces, and other commercial environments that require both style and functionality.

6. Are vinyl floors suitable for high-traffic areas?

Yes, vinyl flooring is known for its resilience and durability, making it an excellent choice for high-traffic areas. It’s also low-maintenance, easy to clean, and available in various designs to suit different spaces..

7. What type of office carpets does Access Floors Trading supply?

We offer premium office carpets, including collections like Belgotex African Skies and Academia. These carpets are designed for durability, comfort, and aesthetic appeal, perfectly suited for professional environments.

9. What industries do you cater to with your flooring solutions?
